Optimize your iProERP Experience with iProERP LearnIt Online

One of the most important aspects to consider when safeguarding your business software investment and ensuring user uptake, is the online ERP training and education of the people who use it. With a focus on continuous learning and improvement, iProERP LearnIt Online is our online learning platform. By providing access to all of iProERP’s training offerings we strive to make your training and learning experience as engaging, relevant and accessible as possible.

CONTACT iProERP

iProERP Learn It Online

As a member of the iProERP community, education plays a vital role in optimizing your iProERP experience and usage. The iProERP LearnIt Online is a one-stop-shop for all your online educational needs, ensuring that you have access to training material when you need it, where you need it.

iProERP Learn It Online provides you with access via your desktop or mobile device to a range of different educational material formats which you can use for self-paced, self-directed learning or to supplement the instructor-led training.

iProERP Learn It Online is an annual subscription that is a cost-effective and complete learning experience that enables companies to measure and monitor the educational progress of each iProERP user. With a iProERP LearnIt Online subscription you can assign users educational pathways leading to their iProERP certifications. It provides organizations and employees the peace of mind that they will receive the opportunity to upskill themselves on the latest iProERP releases as and when they happen, while enabling organizational optimization to be maintained at the highest level.

Benefits of iProERP LearnIt Online

For Learners

  • Become well versed in iProERP and use it to its full potential
  • Stay up-to-date and informed about manufacturing, distribution and Industry 4.0
  • Monitor your own progress.
  • Become part of a community where you can learn and share and obtain a certification.
  • Partners learn about new features and functions to better support their customers.
  • End users learn how to use iProERP to perform their day to day activities efficiently and productivity.

For Companies and Managers

  • Access to a fully functional bespoke learning management system with the facility measure and monitor the progress of each user
  • Providing operational improvements and cost savings through valuable insights into how educational and organizational objectives are being met
  • Educating, informing and training users who will thus improve the efficiency and productivity of your organization

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does iProERP cost?

iProERP is cost-competitive. The cost is based on several factors, including the size of your organization, the number of users and the specific modules you require, customization and deployment method (on-premise or cloud-based) and any additional services including implementation, training, and ongoing support.

What are the requirements for iProERP implementation?

The requirements for implementing iProERP can vary based on your organizational needs, scope of implementation and the deployment method you choose (on-premise or cloud-based). Requirements range from hardware and infrastructure, operating system, licenses, customization, and configuration. To ensure this is as seamless process as possible we have a dedicated support team and trusted advisors who will guide you every step of the way.

How easy is it to implement iProERP ?

Our implementation methodology uses best practices developed over many years of experience and facilitates a successful implementation and a roadmap to reaping a return on investment on the project for many years to come. Implementing iProERP is a scalable, structured, and phased approach consisting of pre-defined inputs, activities and outputs which deliver a solution that meets your objectives.

Can I customize iProERP to fit my unique business needs?

iProERP is highly customizable, enabling businesses to tailor the system to their specific needs. The flexibility means that the software can be adapted to a wide range of industries, and customization of the core solution won’t affect the internal workings of the system.
