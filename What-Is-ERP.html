What is ERP?

Secure your digital supply chain with iProERP

  • Home
  • What is ERP?


ERP is an acronym for Enterprise Resource Planning . ERP is business management software that consists of a suite of integrated applications. ERP software enables businesses to manage and interpret enterprise data. ERP solutions can coordinate the management of multiple business activities such as accounting, operations, supply chain, manufacturing, distribution and human resources. ERP systems automate and integrate core business processes such as taking customer orders, scheduling operations, inventory records, financial data and reporting.

iProERP software is a business performance management system that provides visibility across every aspect of core business processes. The analytics and intelligence allow for one source of truth and enables the digital transformation of your business.

9 Benefits of Implementing an ERP System in Your Business

Today’s manufacturer and distributor face a myriad of business challenges. These include duplication of processes with disparate systems, the inability to support a mobile workforce, scrambling to keep track of resources , departments within the organization working in silos, and the list goes on. Implementing a ProERP system delivers key benefits to assist in overcoming these obstacles.

  • 1. Reduce Costs and Save Money
  • 2. Streamlined Business Processes and Operations
  • 3. Improved Financial Consolidation
  • 4. Supply Chain Optimization
  • 5. Respond to Market Conditions Faster with Data Analysis
  • 6. Traceability
  • 7. Digitally Transform and Future-proof your Business with Mobility and Flexibility
  • 8. Improve Customer Satisfaction, Service and Relations
  • 9. Increase Competitiveness with an Industry Built System

1.

Reduce Costs and Save Money

  • Reduce administrative and operational costs through automated processes. This allows users to pro-actively manage operations and prevent delays.
  • A cut in operational costs is a cut in working capital outlay. An Enterprise Resource Planning System allows you to run your business more cost effectively
  • Reduce waste within your organization, maximize inventory efficiency.

2.

Streamlined Business Processes and Operations

  • Data is available in a centralized location with complete visibility across all functionalities.
  • An ERP System allows you to automate business processes.
  • Decision makers can monitor operations and production in real-time.
  • Automate many tracking processes, and accurately determine and maintain inventory levels.

3.

Improved Financial Consolidation

  • Without ERP, many businesses are forced to use different programs across different departments. An ERP system eliminates the data silos that arise from disparate systems
  • ERP systems enable you to limit labor-intensive tasks doing the work for you.

4.

Supply Chain Optimization

  • A robust ERP System allows for real-time visibility of your supply chain and other processes making it easier for decision makers to get a wider view of their supply chain, reduce planning cycles and be on top of production scheduling.

5.

Respond to Market Conditions Faster with Data Analysis

  • ERP Systems provides real-time data analysis and reporting to assist the business to rapidly react to changing market requirements.
  • The business can make informed decisions on time.
  • Data analysis in ERP software provides access to accurate reports. This allows businesses to follow trends in real-time and determine realistic forecasts.

6.

Traceability

  • Allocation and detection of inventory and stock is critical in highly regulated industries.
  • ERP benefits you in tracing stock, defects and hazards down to the level of individual parts and ingredients.
  • rack materials from receipt right through to delivery of the product to the customer, as well as at any level in between.
  • Improve governance and drive compliance.

7.

Digitally Transform and Future-proof your Business with Mobility and Flexibility

  • As your business grows, your ERP system will grow and adapt to cater for your changing needs.
  • Gain better control over your business with choice in deployment; whether it is on premise, in the cloud (Cloud ERP), or on a Mobile App (Mobile ERP).

8.

Improve Customer Satisfaction, Service and Relations

  • Implementing an ERP solution enables you to produce the right product at the right time. This ensures accuracy in providing for customer needs.
  • On-time deliver is critical in maintaining customer retention and service.

9.

Increase Competitiveness with an Industry Built System

  • An industry built ERP solution allows for configuration to suit industry needs by adapting to individual business requirements.
  • Gain competitive advantage with compliancy, agility and the ability to expand and adapt with little to no downtime.
